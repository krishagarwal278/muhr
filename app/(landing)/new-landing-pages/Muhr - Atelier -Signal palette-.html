Questions

Frequently asked

What is Muhr?
Muhr is a licensing vault and marketplace for your likeness. Brands license your face and voice to create AI-generated video, you approve every use, and you get paid under signed terms. Think of it as stock licensing — for your presence.
Do I need a large following?
No. Muhr is built for on-camera talent and creators across a wide range — roughly 5K to 500K followers. Brands want real, relatable people their audiences trust, not only celebrities.
Can I decline a deal?
Always. You approve the deal before work begins and the final video before it ships — two checkpoints, every time. Decline at either, with no explanation required.
What happens to my data?
Your photos and audio are kept in encrypted, access-controlled storage and used only to produce a specific approved video. We never use your likeness to train AI models, and we never sell your data.
Does it cost anything to join?
No. Creating a profile is free. We take a platform fee only when you complete a paid transaction — you never pay to be listed or to receive license requests.
